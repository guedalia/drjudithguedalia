A Neuropsychologist's Journal

Interventions and “Judi-isms”

by Dr. Judith Bendheim Guedalia · Urim Publications, Jerusalem · ISBN 9789655241235 (hardcover) / 9789655241334 (paperback) · First edition 2012, paperback 2015

Editorial note (from the book's front matter, in her words)

All the names of patients and family members have been changed to protect their privacy. Some identifying details have been changed.

Most of the names of physicians have been changed.

“Chaim K.” is a nom de plume of the author's co-writer in the essays in the section titled “Life-Wizened Chaim K.”

While the articles and essays are divided into sections according to a general topic, there is some overlap of topics. Some essays can be placed in two or even three sections. For example: “When the Deer Hunter Lost” is suitable for the sections on Dates of Sweetness and Awe, Israel in Colorful Times, and Living Torah where it was finally placed.

Nine sections

  1. New Beginnings
  2. Living Torah
  3. Israel in Colorful Times
  4. Joining the Dots
  5. The News: Actors in a Script?
  6. General Information
  7. On Death and Suffering
  8. A Personal Thanksgiving
  9. Life-Wizened Chaim K.

Of the essays archived on this site, 119 are matched to chapters in the book; on each matched essay page, the book section is shown alongside the article.
