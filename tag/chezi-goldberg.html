Tag: Chezi Goldberg

2 articles

Yechezkel “Chezi” Goldberg z"l (b. 1962 Toronto, d. January 5, 2004 Jerusalem) was a journalist, author, and family therapist murdered in a Hamas bus bombing on Egged bus 19 in Jerusalem at age 41. Dr. Guedalia worked with his widow and family in the aftermath and several essays return to his memory and the meaning of his loss.
